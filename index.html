I am a final year PhD student within The Australian Institute for Machine Learning at The University of Adelaide, in South Australia. I work heavily in the fields of Computer Vision, Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence, with my research focused on developing approaches to understand video. I have worked on image and video classification, detection, captioning and tracking with deep neural networks and big datasets.

I am passionate about applying the latest techniques to solve real world problems, as well as effectively communicating ideas to clients, colleagues and the broader community. My time working with industry partners has allowed me the opportunity to implement artificially intelligent soultions to a diversity of interesting problems.
